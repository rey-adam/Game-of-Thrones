The Game of Thrones!

Instructions - "When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die. There is no middle ground."

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game of thrones by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the throne if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each character.
